About me

Biao Ma is a Postdoc Researcher of State Key Laboratory of Digital Medical Engineering at Southeast University at Southeast University, affiliated with the POCT lab (PI: Prof. Hong Liu). Biao received Phd in Biomedical Engineering from Southeast University at 2020. Driven by curiosity, Biao is deeply passionate about cool science discoveries and creating innovative technologies. His current research interest is creating smart matter-based biosensors and bioelectronics for healthcare. Biao has multidisciplinary research experiences in chemistry, materials science, microfabrication, electrochemistry, microfluidics, and device integration. He has published 40 peer-reviewed articles, several of which have been published in journals such as Science Advances, Nature Reviews Bioengineering, Advanced Functional Materials, and Lab on a Chip.
